Psychology: Loss Aversion + Scarcity
Humans feel the pain of losing something 2x more intensely than the pleasure of gaining it (Kahneman & Tversky). This email reframes the decision from "should I book?" to "can I afford to lose this?" The countdown timer adds visceral urgency — they can see time slipping away in real-time.
