Gift Two — The Recipe
The Collins Sunset
Our bartender's signature — now yours to make at home.
Named after the avenue that connects Miami Beach's most iconic spots, The Collins Sunset captures everything we love about golden hour on the terrace — citrus, warmth, and that perfect hint of tropical sweetness. Our head bartender, Marco, created this for guests who wanted to take a piece of the Shelborne bar home.
Ingredients
2 oz aged rum (we use Ron Zacapa 23)
1 oz fresh passion fruit purée
¾ oz fresh lime juice
½ oz honey syrup (2:1 honey to warm water)
2 dashes Angostura bitters
Champagne float (about 1 oz)
Garnish: dehydrated lime wheel + edible flower
Method
1
Combine rum, passion fruit, lime juice, honey syrup, and bitters in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously for 12 seconds.
2
Double strain into a chilled coupe glass.
3
Gently float champagne over the back of a bar spoon so it layers on top.
4
Garnish with a dehydrated lime wheel and an edible flower. Close your eyes. You're back on Collins Avenue.
